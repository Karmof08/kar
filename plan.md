
# Website Plan for Footwear Business

## 1. Objective:
To create a modern, clean, and mobile-friendly website for a footwear business that specializes in shoe repair, shoe polishing, and selling various types of footwear. The website should also facilitate custom orders based on customer-provided images and target students and young adults.

## 2. Target Audience:
Students and young adults.

## 3. Website Pages & Sections:

### 3.1. Home Page:
*   **Hero Section:** Bold headline: "Your Trusted Footwear Plug". Engaging background visuals.
*   **Service Overview:** Short, concise descriptions of shoe repair, polishing, and footwear sales.
*   **Call-to-Action (CTA):** Prominent buttons: "Visit Us" and "Order Now".

### 3.2. Services Page:
*   **Detailed Service Descriptions:** In-depth explanation of shoe repair and polishing processes.
*   **Before-and-After Showcase:** Visuals demonstrating the quality of repair and polishing services.

### 3.3. Shop Page:
*   **Product Categories:** Display footwear categories: Sneakers, Sandals, Corporate Shoes, Slippers.
*   **Product Cards:** Each card should feature an image, name, and price of the footwear.

### 3.4. Custom Orders:
*   **Process Explanation:** Clearly outline how customers can send pictures of desired footwear.
*   **Instructions:** Step-by-step guide for submitting custom order requests.
*   **CTA:** A dedicated "Contact Us for Custom Order" button.

### 3.5. About Page:
*   **Business Story:** A brief narrative about the origins and mission of the business.
*   **Key Values:** Emphasize affordability, quality, and reliability.

### 3.6. Contact Page:
*   **Contact Information:** WhatsApp number, phone number, and physical address (e.g., Room 117, Hostel).
*   **Contact Form:** A simple form for direct inquiries.

## 4. Design Style:
*   **Aesthetic:** Modern, youthful, clean.
*   **Layout:** User-friendly, intuitive navigation.
*   **Color Scheme:** Black, white, with a bold accent color (e.g., orange or gold).
*   **Visuals:** Strong, high-quality images and graphics.

## 5. Extra Features:
*   **Testimonials Section:** Showcase customer reviews and feedback.
*   **Image Gallery:** Display images of past work, repairs, and sold items.
*   **Mobile Optimization:** Fully responsive design for seamless viewing on all devices.

## 6. Navigation:
*   Simple and clear top navigation bar linking to all main pages.
*   Footer with essential links and contact information.

## 7. Implementation Notes:
*   Prioritize mobile-first design.
*   Ensure fast loading times.
*   Use clear and concise language.
